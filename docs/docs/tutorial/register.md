# How to Register

This guide walks you through creating a new G-RANK account.

---

## Prerequisites

- A valid email address you have access to
- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge, Safari)

---

## Step 1 — Go to the Register Page

Navigate to the G-RANK platform and click **Register** in the top navigation bar, or go directly to `/register`.

---

## Step 2 — Fill in Your Details

You will see a registration form with the following fields:

| Field | Requirements |
|---|---|
| **Username** | Unique, no spaces recommended |
| **Email** | Valid email address, must be unique |
| **Password** | Must meet minimum security requirements |
| **Confirm Password** | Must match the Password field |

:::tip
Choose a memorable username — it will be displayed publicly on the leaderboard and in lobbies.
:::

---

## Step 3 — Submit the Form

Click **Create Account**. If all fields are valid, the server will:

1. Create your account with the default **USER** role
2. Assign a starting MMR of **0** (Bronze tier)
3. Send a verification email to the address you provided

You will see a success message:

> **Registration successful!**
> Please check your email inbox and click the verification link to activate your account.

---

## Step 4 — Verify Your Email

Open your email inbox and look for a message from `G-Rank <grank.noreply@gmail.com>`.

Click the **Verify Email** button or link in the email. This will redirect you to the G-RANK platform with a confirmation message.

:::info
The verification link expires after a set period. If it expires, you can request a new one from the Login page.
:::

---

## Step 5 — Log In

After verifying your email, you can now [log in](./login.md) with your email and password.

---

## Troubleshooting

### I did not receive the verification email

1. Check your spam/junk folder.
2. Wait a few minutes — email delivery can occasionally be delayed.
3. Go to the Login page and click **Resend verification email**.

### My username or email is already taken

Each username and email must be unique across the platform. Try a different username or use a different email address.

### The form shows a validation error

- **Password mismatch** — ensure both password fields are identical.
- **Weak password** — use a stronger password (mix of letters, numbers, and symbols).
- **Invalid email** — check that the email address is properly formatted.
